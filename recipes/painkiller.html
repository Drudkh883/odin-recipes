Pain Killer

  1. 1 ounce cream of coconut
  2. 1 ounce orange juice
  3. 2 ounces dark rum
  4. 4 ounces pineapple juice

Directions

This drink will be built in a shaker with cubed ice.

After measuring each of the following add them to the shaker.

Step 1

Fill measuring the shot glass with 1 ounce cream of coconut and 1 ounce orange juice

Step 2

2 - 3 ounces dark rum

Step 3

4 ounces of pineapple juice

Step 4

Shake vigorously for 15 seconds. We want FOAM!

Strain into a rocks glass filled with crushed ice.

Garnish with grated nutmeg.
