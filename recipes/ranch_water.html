Ranch Water

  • Ice
  • 3 oz tequila
  • 1.5 oz fresh lime juice
  • Chilled Topo Chico
  • Lime wedge for garnish

Steps

  1. Add ice to a glass (Literally any glass you have)
  2. Measure 3 oz tequila, add it to that glass with ice
  3. Add 1.5 oz or one half squeezed lime
  4. Top the glass off with Topo Chico and garnish with lime wedge
